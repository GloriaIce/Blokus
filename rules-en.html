Rule of BLOKUS

To win the Blokus game:
The player's goal in this game is to place all or maximum of their 21 pieces on the board. At the end of the game, the player who occupies more blocks wins.

Rules of Blokus:
1. The first piece of first player mush occupy the top-left block, and the second player much occupy bottom-right corner.
